About Me

During my time at Appalachian State University I was introduced to Geography and the power that it has for understanding the world that we live in. I'm passionate about leveraging Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and spatial analysis to explore complex patterns, particularly in environmental and health-related contexts. I enjoy the entire process, from field data collection using technologies like UAVs to processing, analysis, and creating compelling data visualizations.
